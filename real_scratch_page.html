How to play ultimate

Common Questions

  1. How many players do I need?
      Regulation Ultimate is played with 7 players on each team, but a good game can usually be maintained with 4-10 people per team.
  2. Size of field
      The field is 120 yards in length and 40 yards wide
  3. What equipment do I need to play?
      All you will need is a disc and 8 cones. Most serious players use soccer cleats for extra traction, but they are not necessary.
  4. How do you play?
      Your objective is to catch the frisbee in the endzone of the opposing team. When you are holding the frisbee, you cannot move, so the only way to move the disc forward is by throwing to a teammate.
  5. How does possession of the frisbee change?
      If the frisbee hits the ground or the other team catches it, they now have possession.
